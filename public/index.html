MyCia Token (MCT) is a token that runs on the Binance Smart Chain and serves as fuel powering the Crypto Insights Academy (CIA) ecosystem, a complete DeFi ecosystem fully focused on a decent learning and earning environment within the Cryptocurrency and financial market trading.

Use Cases

Pay rewards and rebates to members of the Crypto Academy Community, a learning platform aimed towards mass adoption of Cryptocurrencies.

Settle fees and rebates on the CIA Peer-to-peer marketplace.

To power the CIA trading bot (also known as MCT bot).

MCT Burnout Mechanism

Every quarter, MyCia uses 10% of its earnings from signup fees/ Academy registrations and other fees to buy-back and burn MCT, destroying them completely from circulation. MyCia will consistently perform these quarterly burns from the first day of its listing.

July 26, 202200:00 Hours GMT

Burnout activation

Members of CIA can buy $100 or $200 worth of MCT for burnout on MyCia then earn 30% or 50% MCT respectively from the burnout MCT of anyone under their referral link who conducts burnout on MyCia platform. MyCia will use 15% of the purchased burnout to pay everyone equally on the MyCia burnout platform, use 20% of the purchased MCT burnout to conduct its quarterly burnout, 40% to pay referrers while the remaining MCT will be used for the maintenance of the ecosystem.

July 16th, 202200:00 Hours GMT

MCT Trading bot

In the operation of the MCT trading bot, users will be expected to hold a fixed amount of MCT to power the bot. As the bot runs, the MCT will gradually be used to pay for fees which eventually will deplete and the user will be expected to deposit another fixed amount of MCT to power the bot. 45% of every depleted MCT will be used for the upgrading of the trading bot while 10% will be distributed to all users in the community and the rest will be added to the quarterly MCT burnout.

July 26, 202200:00 Hours GMT

Lending and Liquidity Pool

As a decentralised finance protocol, users can Stake, Save and Provide liquidity on the MyCia platform and earn rewards: Staking: earn up to 30% APY in MCT fixed or flexible

Alongside with the users’ burnout, every quarter, MyCia uses 10% of ALL its earnings: from signup fees/ Academy registrations and other fees to buy-back and burn MCT, destroying them completely from circulation. MyCia will consistently perform these quarterly burns until it buys back and destroys 12,000,000 MCT – 50% of the total supply. This practice ensures that the supply of MCT remains finite, making it scarce and more valuable.
